The Blinds

Day / Night Style

Day / night style cellular blinds consist of two different honeycomb materials; total blackout and semi-transparent privacy screening. This provides more lighting control in your living space.

  • The blackout material is internally foil lined for optimal insulation. Available in different colours. See Colours.
  • Blinds will be made to tailor fit your home with minimum air-gaps in mind to achieve maximum thermal benefits.
  • The metal pull cord can be placed on either the right or left side of the window.
  • Blinds can be hung either internally or external to window depending your requirments.
  • Maximum width and height is 2100mm x 2100mm.
  • An 18 month warranty is provided with all products and installation.
