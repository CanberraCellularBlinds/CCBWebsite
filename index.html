The Blinds

Two Styles:

  • Day/Night Style
  • Blockout Style

Day / Night Cellular Style

Day/Night style cellular blinds consist of two different honeycomb materials; the coloured blockout screening and semi-transparent privacy screening. This dynamic lighting control in your living space.

  • The blackout material is internally foil lined to limit heat exchange, increasing the r-value of the blind.
  • You choose the colour of the Blockout section of the blind. See Colours.
  • The semi-transparent privacy screening lacks the insulation properties of the blockout material but allows airflow and light in to adapt to changing climate conditions thoughout the year and the day.
  • The metal ball-chain pull cord can be placed on either the right or left side of the blind to ergonomicaly adapt to the living space.
  • Blinds can be hung either internal or external to window cavity depending requirments.
  • Maximum width and height is 2100mm x 3000mm. Longer blinds can be split into sections.
  • A 12 month warranty is provided with our products.

Blockout Cellular Blind

The blockout cellular blind solely consit of the Blockout section of the Day/Night blind. Due to it being more binary in nature it is more suitable for quieter living spaces in the home or if windows are to be blocked out for longer durations.
Great for blocking out large spaces at a budget friendly price and proiding a modern asthetic.

  • The blackout material is internally foil lined to limit heat exchange, increasing the r-value of the blind.
  • You choose the colour of the Blockout section of the blind. See Colours.
  • The metal ball-chain pull cord can be placed on either the right or left side of the blind to ergonomicaly adapt to the living space.
  • Blinds can be hung either internal or external to window depending your requirments.
  • Maximum width and height is 2100mm x 2100mm. Longer blinds can be split into sections.
  • An 12 month warranty is provided with our products.
